Prerequisites

🐍
Python & PyTorch Basics

Comfortable writing Python functions and familiar with tensor operations in PyTorch or NumPy. You don't need to have trained models from scratch.

📐
Basic Linear Algebra

Understanding of vectors, matrices, dot products, and matrix multiplication. Familiarity with concepts like eigenvectors is helpful but not required.

🔭
Curiosity About Models

The most important prerequisite. A genuine desire to understand what happens inside neural networks — not just how to use them, but how they actually compute.
